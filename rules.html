New Divsions

The new divisions for the second half of the year have been decided:

  • Top Division:Mario, Luigi, Wario, Peach
  • Bottom Division:Yoshi, Toad, Daisy, Waluigi

    • Players will play only in their division. Players in the bottom division will start with three stars every game.

Rules

  • 10 Battle Roayle (2 on each board)
  • 10 Tag Battles (2 on each board)
  • 20 Duel Battles (4 on each board)
  • 3 Score Scuffles
  • 3 Battle Cups
  • 3 Step it Ups
  • 3 Rocket Rascals
  • 52 Total Games
  • Standings Determined By Points System
    Win +5 Points, Stars +3 Points, Minigame Wins +2 Points, Coins +1 Point
  • Season Divided Into Two Divisions At Halfway Point (Top 4 Players and Top 4)
  • Divisions Updated After Every Four Games(Before Players Switch Boards)
