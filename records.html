Season will resume with Rocket Rascals on Friday, April 2nd

Mario Party will return on Friday, after the final negative test of Toad. All competitors will be forced to follow strict protocols for the remainder of the year and will have to quarantine for the entirety of the week until Friday, in order to ensure play continues with no stoppage of schedule.


Schedule Changes

The one week break that was set for after Bowser's Pinball Machine is now removed: players will move straight to DK's Stone Statue after the conclusion of the fifth board's play
