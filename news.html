Potential outcomes for the Season

Although nothing is official as of now and all should expect the season to continue, the outlook for the year continuing is actively decreasing. Three possible outcomes have been discused:

  • Crowning Mario as champ(leading league)
  • Have top two play series for championship
  • Outwait positive tests

Season will resume with Rocket Rascals on Wednesday, March 31st

Mario Party will return on Wednesday, after the final negative test of Toad. All competitors will be forced to follow strict protocols for the remainder of the year and will have to quarantine for the entirety of the week until Friday, in order to ensure play continues with no stoppage of schedule.
